About Spotify & Disruptive Innovation

Spotify is a leading digital music streaming service. As discussed in Part 1 of our project, Spotify represents Clayton Christensen's Disruptive Innovation Model. It disrupted the traditional physical record market (CDs/Vinyl) and early digital downloading models (like iTunes) by offering a subscription-based, on-demand streaming service.

This program mimics its core algorithm by recommending playlists based on user preferences, genre, mood, and listening habits.
