About Spotify & Disruptive Innovation

How Spotify changed the music industry forever.

What is Spotify?

Spotify is a leading digital music streaming service founded in 2006 in Stockholm, Sweden. It offers on-demand access to millions of songs, podcasts, and playlists across all devices.

Clayton Christensen's Disruptive Innovation Model

As discussed in Part 1 of our project, Spotify is a textbook example of Clayton Christensen's Disruptive Innovation Model. It entered the market at a lower price point and simpler experience, then rapidly improved to dominate.

  • Replaced physical CDs and vinyl records.
  • Disrupted digital downloads like iTunes with subscription streaming.
  • Introduced data-driven personalisation at massive scale.
  • Made music globally accessible on any device, anytime.
  • Grew from startup to over 600 million users worldwide.

Connection to This Program

This recommendation engine mimics Spotify's core algorithm — using user data (genre, mood, listening habits) to deliver personalised playlists. It directly reflects the innovation life cycle explored in our Part 1 poster.
